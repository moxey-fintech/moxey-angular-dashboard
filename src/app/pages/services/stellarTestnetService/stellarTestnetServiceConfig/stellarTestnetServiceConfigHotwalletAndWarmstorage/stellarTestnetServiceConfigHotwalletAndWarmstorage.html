Hot wallet & Warm storage
  • Hot wallet
  • A hot wallet is a transactional account/float that is used to send transactions on behalf of your users. It exists to minimize risk and allows you to only keep a small amount of your total cryptocurrency value in a “live” state at any one point in time.
  • The Stellar Testnet Extension has generated and funded a Stellar testnet account on your behalf to act as your company hot wallet.
  • Warm storage
  • Warm storage is a deposit account on the Stellar testnet network. If you want users to be able to deposit or move funds onto your system, it's best to setup a warm storage.
  • Rehive has generated a Warm storage account using your Hot wallet's public address.
