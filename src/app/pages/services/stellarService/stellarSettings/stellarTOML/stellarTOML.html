TOML File

The stellar.toml file contains general public information on your company and assets that might be required by the larger Stellar ecosystem. To read more view this article.


Rehive generates a stellar.toml file based on your company assets and Rehive company details. It is best to double check the data that is generated before adding the file to your domain. Once checked the file should be uploaded to https://COMPANY_DOMAIN/.well-known/stellar.toml that is controlled by your company.
