Hot wallet
  • A hot wallet is a transactional account/float that is used to send transactions on behalf of your users. It exists to minimize risk and allows you to only keep a small amount of your total cryptocurrency value in a “live” state at any one point in time. It can also be used as your warm storage (not recommended).
  • The extension has generated a Stellar account on your behalf to act as your company hot wallet. In order to activate the account you must fund your hot wallet.
  • To fund your hot wallet please make a transaction to the address on the right.
