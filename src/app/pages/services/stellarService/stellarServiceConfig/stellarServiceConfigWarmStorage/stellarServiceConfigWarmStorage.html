Warm storage
  • Warm storage is a deposit account on the Stellar network. If you want users to be able to deposit or move funds onto your system, it's best to setup a warm storage. Rehive keeps track of the balance and transactions associated with the account.
  • You will need an active account on the Stellar network. Active accounts are accounts with at least 1 XLM in them. You can create an account via one of the wallets here.
  • Create an account on the Stellar network by generating a keypair.
  • You will receive a Public Key and a Secret Key. Store the Secret Key somewhere safe. If this key is lost you will lose the funds.
  • Fund the account with at least 1 XLM. Confirm on the right when your account is funded.
  • Add your Public Key to Rehive so its transactions can be tracked. Rehive does not have access to the funds.
